# Mothership System Principles

- Create situations, not prescribed plots.
- Preserve meaningful player agency.
- Treat information as a major reward.
- Build threats and factions that act without the player characters.
- Use uncertainty, isolation, scarcity, Stress, and consequence as pressure.
- Do not require one clue, NPC, scene, or outcome for progression.
- Combat is dangerous and not a balanced default solution.
- Mysteries may remain partially unresolved.
